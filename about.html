Who we are

Antonio Venucci

At Antonio Venucci, we are dedicated to providing our customers with the highest quality leather footwear and accessories. We pride ourselves on our end-to-end solutions, ensuring that every step of the manufacturing process is seamless and tailored to the specific needs of our customers.
We take great care in the craftsmanship of our products, infusing precision and attention to detail in every design. Our team of skilled artisans is dedicated to creating premium leather goods that are not only stylish but also built to last. We understand that each customer has unique needs and preferences, that's why we offer customization options to ensure that you get exactly what you want.

From the initial design phase to the final delivery, we strive to provide a smooth and easy experience for our customers. We believe in transparency throughout the manufacturing process and are always happy to share our process with our customers.

We are dedicated to crafting the finest leather footwear and accessories that our customers will cherish. Explore our collection and appreciate the quality and attention to detail that goes into every piece we create.

Our vision

To create a smooth experience for customers looking for premium leather footwear and accessories, offering end-to-end solutions, seamless process, expert craftsmanship, and personalization options

Our values
What matters to us

At our company, we are guided by three essential principles: accountability, attention to detail, and strong relationships. These values shape our culture and drive us to offer exceptional service and products to our clients, partners and employees.

Accountability

Our team of designers work closely with our production team to bring the latest trends to our customers, while ensuring that our manufacturing process is ethical, efficient and sustainable. Your satisfaction is our top priority and we strive to deliver your order on time, every time.

Attention to detail

From the initial design concept to the finished product, we pay close attention to every detail, ensuring that our customers receive high-quality, stylish and durable leather shoes and accessories.

Strong relationships

We believe in open and frequent communication, always available to address any concerns or issues that may arise. Honesty and transparency are at the heart of all of our interactions, and we believe that this approach is the key to building successful and long lasting relationships.

Sustainability
Every Step Matters

We are committed to sustainability and ethical manufacturing practices. We source materials from sustainable and ethical suppliers, while having a strict code of conduct for our manufacturing facilities.

Vegan Leather

An environmental friendly and ethical alternative to traditional animal leather that doesn't involve harm to animals.

Recycling

We are dedicated to protecting the environment by implementing recycling programs for materials used in the production and manufacturing process.

Minimal Wastage

We implement a “closed-loop” production system where waste materials are repurposed or recycled within the production process.

Green Energy

We are committed to using green energy to power 70% percentage of our production facilities, offices and warehouses by 2025.

Social Cause

At Antonio Venucci, we are committed to supporting marginalized and underrepresented groups in the community by providing training and nurturing them into artisans. Our goal is to empower these individuals to not only earn a livelihood but also to provide for their families.

Certifications

All our manufacturing facilities hold International is an ISO 9001, ISO 14001, SA-8000-2014 (BSI), SEDEX & SMETA certification.
