Design and Concept Development

This involves the initial stage of creating designs and developing concepts for your leather footwear and accessories.

Material Selection and Sourcing

We carefully choose the materials required for manufacturing our products and ethically source them from reliable suppliers.

Pattern Making and Grading

Patterns are created based on the designs, and grading is done to ensure that the sizes and proportions are consistent across different product variations.

Cutting and Stitching

The selected materials are cut according to the patterns, and skilled artisans stitch them together to form the desired footwear or accessory.

Finishing and Quality Control

After the assembly process, finishing touches are added to the products, and a thorough quality control check is conducted to ensure they meet the standards.

Packaging and Shipping

Once the products pass the quality control stage, they are carefully packaged to ensure their protection during transportation.

Delivery and Customer Service

The packaged products are shipped to the customers, and we provide dedicated customer service to address any queries or concerns you may have.

Frequently asked questions

Have a question? Check out our FAQ section for answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about our products and services.

Contact us

Do you offer custom manufacturing services?

Yes, we offer custom manufacturing services for clients with specific design and material requirements.

Do you offer private label options?

Yes, we offer private label options for clients who want to brand their own products.

How can I explore your catalogs?

You can explore our products from the Men and Women pages. Please fill out inquiry form through
'Get in Touch' to get exclusive access key.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies depending on the product and the manufacturing process.
Please contact us for more information.

What materials do you use in your products?

Our products are made with a variety of materials including leather, suede, and various fabrics.

What is your lead time for production?

Lead times for production vary depending on the complexity of the order.
We will provide an estimated lead time once an order has been placed.

How do you ensure quality control in your products?

We have a strict quality control process in place. We inspect our products at every stage of the
manufacturing process and conduct final inspections before shipping.

Can you provide samples of your products?

Yes, we can provide samples of our products. Please get in touch with us.

What are the shipping options available?

We offer various shipping options, including air freight, sea freight, and land transportation.
The specific option will depend on your destination, budget, and urgency of delivery.

How are shipping costs determined?

Shipping costs are determined based on various factors, such as the weight, dimensions, destination,
shipping method, and any additional services required (e.g., insurance, customs clearance).
We can provide you with a detailed cost breakdown for your specific shipment.

Can I request expedited shipping?

Yes, we offer expedited shipping services for urgent shipments. Please let us know your requirements,
and we will provide you with the best available options and associated costs.

How can I get in touch with your customer service team?

You can contact our customer service team through live chat, email, phone or through our
website contact form. We are happy to answer any questions you may have.
