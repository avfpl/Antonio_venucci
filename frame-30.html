Frequently asked questions

Have a question? Check out our FAQ section for answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about our products and services.

Contact us

Do you offer custom manufacturing services?

Yes, we offer custom manufacturing services for clients with specific design and material requirements.

Do you offer private label options?

Yes, we offer private label options for clients who want to brand their own products.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies depending on the product and the manufacturing process. Please contact us for more information

What materials do you use in your products?

Our products are made with a variety of materials including leather, suede, and various fabrics.

What is your lead time for production?

Lead times for production vary depending on the complexity of the order. We will provide an estimated lead time once an order has been placed.

How do you ensure quality control in your products?

We have a strict quality control process in place. We inspect our products at every stage of the manufacturing process and conduct final inspections before shipping.

Can you provide samples of your products?

Yes, we can provide samples of our products. Please get in touch with us.

How long will it take for my order to ship and additional costs?

Our standard shipping time is 3-5 business days from the date of order completion. However, during high volume periods, such as holidays, shipping may take longer. Shipping costs vary depending on your location and the size of your order. We will provide you with an estimate cost for shipping when placing an order.
